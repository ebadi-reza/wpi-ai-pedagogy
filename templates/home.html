Our Mission

The AI Pedagogy initiative at WPI helps faculty navigate and shape the future of teaching and learning in the age of AI. We are committed to:

  • Supporting academic integrity while thoughtfully redesigning assignments and assessments.
  • Building faculty expertise in AI ethics, critical literacy, and responsible use.
  • Creating inclusive opportunities for all faculty to explore AI, no matter their discipline or starting point.
  • Staying future-focused by keeping the WPI community informed about emerging trends and tools.

This work is rooted in partnership with faculty expertise across disciplines and guided by our shared commitment to thoughtful, ethical, and student-centered teaching.
