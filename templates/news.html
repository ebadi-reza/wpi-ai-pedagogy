October 3, 2025

AI Pedagogy Platform Launches at WPI

We're excited to announce the launch of the AI Pedagogy platform at Worcester Polytechnic Institute. This new resource hub will serve as a central location for faculty and students to access materials, share experiences, and learn about best practices for integrating AI into education.

The platform features curated resources, case studies from WPI courses, and regular updates about AI education initiatives on campus. We invite all members of the WPI community to explore the resources and join us in shaping the future of AI-enhanced learning.

September 30, 2025

WPI Faculty AI Baseline Survey Results Released

Thank you to the 155 faculty who participated in the AI Baseline Survey this fall. The results show that nearly half of respondents use AI tools regularly or frequently, while the majority identify as beginners or intermediates in skill level. Faculty expressed the greatest need for support around ethics and academic integrity, classroom activities, and assignment redesign.

These insights will guide upcoming programming, including our first AI Pedagogy Lab on Ethics and Academic Integrity on October 9. Together, we are building a thoughtful, ethical, and practical approach to AI at WPI.

AI Pedagogy Specialist Position Launches at WPI

Prof. Ebadi is appointed as the AI Pedagogy Specialist at the Morgan Center for the academic year 2025-26. With a focus on foundational knowledge, problem-solving, and emerging technologies, he's leading campus-wide conversations on the responsible use of AI in education.
