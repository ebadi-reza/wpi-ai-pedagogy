About AI Pedagogy at WPI

The AI Pedagogy initiative at Worcester Polytechnic Institute is a Morgan Center program dedicated to supporting thoughtful, ethical integration of artificial intelligence in teaching and learning.

Meet the AI Pedagogy Specialist

Reza Ebadi

Assistant Professor of Teaching
Mechanical & Materials Engineering
AI Pedagogy Specialist, Morgan Center

Dr. Reza Ebadi is an Assistant Professor of Teaching in Mechanical & Materials Engineering and the AI Pedagogy Specialist with the Morgan Center. His teaching philosophy centers on balancing essential knowledge delivery, problem-solving skill development, and the thoughtful integration of emerging technologies such as artificial intelligence.

Through his role as AI Pedagogy Specialist, Dr. Ebadi fosters a campus-wide conversation about the responsible and effective use of AI in teaching and learning, creating spaces for faculty and students to experiment, share insights, and build practical resources together.

Our Mission

WPI is advancing two connected priorities around AI: supporting "AI for all" through curriculum integration and fostering AI-driven pedagogical change. This initiative reflects our commitment to doing this work thoughtfully, ethically, and in partnership with the expertise faculty bring from their disciplines.

What We Offer

  • One-on-one consultations to help you redesign assignments, integrate AI tools, and develop pedagogical strategies tailored to your course and discipline.

  • Monthly sessions on a specific AI theme in teaching. These sessions provide a supportive, judgment-free space to share concerns and strategies with colleagues.

  • Practical discussions focused on real classroom applications, ethical considerations, and pedagogical strategies that work.

  • Access to guides, videos, templates, and tested resources designed specifically for busy faculty across all disciplines.

  • Connect with fellow faculty exploring AI pedagogy, share experiences, and learn from each other's successes and challenges.

  • Stay informed about the latest developments in AI and education through curated updates that cut through the hype.
