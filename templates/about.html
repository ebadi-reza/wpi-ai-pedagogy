About AI Pedagogy at WPI

The AI Pedagogy initiative at Worcester Polytechnic Institute is dedicated to exploring and advancing the integration of artificial intelligence in educational contexts.

Our Mission

We aim to empower educators and students with the knowledge, tools, and resources needed to effectively leverage AI technologies in teaching and learning. Our platform serves as a hub for sharing best practices, research findings, and innovative approaches to AI-enhanced education.
